What is Runway ML?

Runway ML is a pioneering AI-powered video generation and editing platform that democratizes content creation. With its flagship Gen-3 Alpha model, Runway enables creators to generate high-quality videos from text prompts, edit existing footage with AI, and access professional-grade tools for filmmaking and content production.

Key Features

  • Gen-3 Alpha: State-of-the-art text-to-video generation model
  • Image to Video: Transform static images into dynamic video content
  • Video Editing Suite: AI-powered editing tools and effects
  • Motion Control: Precise control over camera movements and object motion
  • Style Transfer: Apply artistic styles to video content
