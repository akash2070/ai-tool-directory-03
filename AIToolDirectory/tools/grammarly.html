What is Grammarly?

Grammarly is the world's leading AI-powered writing assistant that helps millions of users write clearly and effectively. It provides real-time grammar checking, style suggestions, tone detection, and plagiarism detection across multiple platforms and applications.

  • Grammar & Spelling: Advanced error detection and correction
  • Style & Clarity: Suggestions for clearer, more engaging writing
  • Tone Detection: Analyze and adjust your writing tone
  • Plagiarism Checker: Detect potential plagiarism issues
  • Browser Extension: Works across all websites and platforms
