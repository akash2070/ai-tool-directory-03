What is Copy.ai?

Copy.ai is an AI-powered copywriting platform that helps marketers, entrepreneurs, and content creators generate high-converting copy for various marketing channels. With over 90 templates and tools, it streamlines content creation for social media, emails, ads, and website copy.

Key Features

  • 90+ Templates: Extensive library covering all marketing content types
  • Multi-language Support: Generate content in 25+ languages
  • Brand Voice: Maintain consistent brand messaging across content
  • Team Collaboration: Share projects and collaborate with team members
  • Chrome Extension: Generate copy directly in your browser
