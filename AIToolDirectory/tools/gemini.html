What is Google Gemini?

Google Gemini is Google's most advanced multimodal AI assistant, designed to understand and generate text, images, audio, and video content. Built from the ground up for multimodal understanding, Gemini represents Google's flagship AI offering that integrates seamlessly with Google's ecosystem of products and services.

Key Features

  • Multimodal Understanding: Process text, images, audio, and video in a single conversation
  • Google Integration: Seamless connection to Gmail, Drive, YouTube, and other Google services
  • Real-time Information: Access to current information through Google Search integration
  • Code Generation: Advanced programming assistance across multiple languages
  • Creative Capabilities: Writing, brainstorming, and creative content generation
