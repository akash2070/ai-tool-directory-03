What is Stable Diffusion?

Stable Diffusion is a revolutionary open-source AI image generation model that runs locally on consumer hardware. Developed by Stability AI, it offers complete freedom to generate unlimited images without usage restrictions, while supporting extensive customization through fine-tuned models, LoRAs, and community extensions.

Key Features

  • Open Source: Completely free with full source code access and modification rights
  • Local Hosting: Run entirely on your own hardware with complete privacy
  • Custom Models: Thousands of specialized models for different styles and subjects
  • Advanced Controls: Precise generation control with ControlNet, inpainting, and upscaling
  • No Restrictions: Unlimited generations without content filters or usage limits
