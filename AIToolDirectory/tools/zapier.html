What is Zapier?

Zapier is the world's leading workflow automation platform that connects over 5,000 apps and services to automate repetitive tasks without coding. It enables businesses and individuals to create automated workflows called "Zaps" that trigger actions across different applications, saving time and reducing manual work.

Key Features

  • 5,000+ App Integrations: Connect virtually any business app or service
  • No-Code Automation: Create workflows without programming knowledge
  • Multi-Step Zaps: Complex workflows with conditional logic and branching
  • AI-Powered Features: Smart suggestions and automation optimization
  • Team Collaboration: Share and manage automations across teams
