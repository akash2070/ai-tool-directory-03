What is Notion AI?

Notion AI combines the power of Notion's comprehensive workspace platform with integrated artificial intelligence capabilities. It offers a unified solution for note-taking, project management, databases, and collaboration, enhanced by AI features that help with writing, summarizing, brainstorming, and organizing information within your workspace.

Key Features

  • AI Writing Assistant: Generate, edit, and improve content directly within your workspace
  • All-in-One Platform: Notes, databases, wikis, and project management in one place
  • Collaborative Workspace: Real-time collaboration with team members and guests
  • Database Integration: Powerful relational databases with AI-enhanced data management
  • Template Library: Extensive collection of pre-built templates for various use cases
